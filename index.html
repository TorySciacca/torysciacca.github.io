  • About

    • Below is a list of my favourite albums in the year they came out, chosen at the dawn of the new year. The albums are listed in alphabetical order and there is no ranking apart from the fact they made the list.

    I started these lists back in 2018 to join the online trend of music chart posting that sparked discussions across the web and personally gave me an endless amount of new music to listen to based on albums that I loved and enjoyed. This trend has fallen out of fashion but it created a wave of people that share a taste in music that was strangely connected. Bands such as Death Grips thrived on these sorts of music sharing trends via the internet. In my own tiny but passionate way I want to keep that torch burning as well as continue to catalogue and reflect on the music that impacts me. Year in, year out.
